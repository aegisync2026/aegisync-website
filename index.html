About Aegisync

Building Reliable, Secure, and
Scalable Digital Infrastructure

Aegisync Limited is a Hong Kong-registered technology company delivering regional and global ICT, network, and software solutions for enterprises across APAC and beyond.

We provide comprehensive capabilities including data center and colocation services, network monitoring platforms, custom software development, enterprise ICT integration, and technical talent outsourcing. Our DIA connectivity adn SD-WAN solution is currently under development and scheduled for launch by the end of this year.

With deep expertise in ICT and network engineering, we help businesses build stable, secure, and scalable digital infrastructure that supports their global operations.
